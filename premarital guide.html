Parents are primarily responsible for protecting children from sexual abuse,
not the other way round. Parents have the power and strength of knowledge children don't have. Remember you can't protect a child from sexual wolves in the present age without paying a price. The Enduring panacea to the Frightening reality of abuse is for parent to seek available information and updated skill on this subject. This is surely a small price to pay compared to the traumatic experience of being a victim.
